# Speed Boat Booking App

## Vision
A premium speed boat rental platform connecting adventure-seekers with boat owners. Users browse available boats, view details, and book experiences with confidence. Designed for coastal vacation markets.

## Design
**Color Palette:**
- `--primary: 220 40% 20%` (deep navy)
- `--background: 40 30% 96%` (warm sand/cream)
- `--foreground: 220 25% 15%` (dark slate)
- `--accent: 200 90% 50%` (bright azure)
- `--muted: 220 20% 90%` (light cool grey)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans (600, 700)
- Body: Work Sans (400, 500)

**Style:** Clean, photography-forward, generous spacing, elevated card designs with subtle shadows

## Features
1. **Boat Browse** — Grid of available boats with photos, pricing, capacity
2. **Booking Form** — Date selection, passenger count, contact details
3. **Boat Details** — Full specs, photo gallery, owner info, booking CTA
4. **User Dashboard** — View upcoming/past bookings
