# Maldives Shop POS System

## Vision
A streamlined point-of-sale system designed for small shops in the Maldives. Enables shop owners to manage products, track inventory, and process sales efficiently. Built for ease of use with staff who may have varying technical expertise.

## Design
**Color Palette:**
- `--primary: 180 60% 35%` (ocean teal)
- `--background: 0 0% 100%` (clean white)
- `--foreground: 200 25% 15%` (dark slate)
- `--accent: 25 85% 55%` (coral orange)
- `--muted: 200 15% 95%` (light grey)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans (600, 700)
- Body: Work Sans (400, 500)

**Style:** Clean, functional, high-contrast for readability, card-based layouts, clear touch targets for tablet use

## Features
1. **Product Catalog** — Browse, search, add/edit products with pricing and SKU
2. **Checkout Flow** — Shopping cart, quantity adjustment, payment processing, receipt generation
3. **Inventory Management** — Track stock levels, low-stock alerts, restock interface
4. **Sales Dashboard** — Daily sales overview, transaction history
